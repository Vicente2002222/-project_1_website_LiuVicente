Lebron James Championships

Lebron James is an professional basketball player of the Cleveland Cavaliers of the NBA. James has won three NBA championships,four NBA Most Valuable Player Awards, three NBA Finals MVP Awards,two Olympic gold medals,an NBA scoring title,and three NBA Finals MVP Awards.He also been selected to the 12 NBA all star team twice.

Lebron James Early Life

James was born on December 30,1984 in Akron Ohio, to a 16 years old mother, Gloria Marie James.Growing up,life was often, a struggle for James family.They always moved from apartment to apartment. James and his friends Sian Cotton, Dru Joyce III, and Willie McGee.Inseparable, they dubbed themselves the "Fab Four" and promised each other that they would attend high school together. .
