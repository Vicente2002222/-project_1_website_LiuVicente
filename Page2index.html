James Profile

Many basketball coaches,fans and current and former players consider James to be one of the greatest player of all-time. James standing at 6 feet 8 inches and weighing 250 pounds,James has started small forward and power forward,he also can play the other three positions. James considered one of the most athletic player in the NBA history,and has been compared to Hall of Farmers Oscar Robertson, Magic johnson,and Michael Jordan.While James has never won the Defensive Player of the Year Award, he has finished second in the voting twice and lists it as one of his main goals.

James business interests

James has taken a unique approach to his playing contracts, usually opting to sign shorter term deals in order to maximize his earnings potential and flexibility. In 2006, he and the Cavaliers negotiated a three-year, $60 million contract extension instead of the four year maximum as it allotted him the option of seeking a new contract worth more money as an unrestricted free agent following the 2010 season. James took less money in order for Miami to be able to afford Chris Bosh and Dwyane Wade as well as further roster support. In 2014, he rejoined Cleveland on a two-year contract worth $42.1 million with an option to become a free agent again in 2015. The next offseason, he opted out of the contract and re-signed with the Cavaliers on another two-year contract with a player option for the second year.
